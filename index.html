Professional Radon Testing

Protect your family with accurate radon level detection

Why Radon Testing Matters

Radon is a colorless, odorless radioactive gas that can seep into your home from the ground. It's the second leading cause of lung cancer in the United States, responsible for about 21,000 deaths annually.

Our Testing Process

We use EPA-approved testing methods to provide accurate radon level measurements:

  • Professional-grade continuous radon monitors
  • 48-72 hour testing period for accurate results
  • Detailed report with recommendations
  • Follow-up consultation included

When to Test

  • Before buying or selling a home
  • After home renovations
  • Every 2 years for ongoing monitoring
  • If you've never tested your home

Radon Mitigation Systems

Effective solutions to reduce radon levels in your home

Professional Radon Mitigation

If your home tests above 4.0 pCi/L, the EPA recommends installing a radon mitigation system. Our certified technicians design and install custom systems to effectively reduce radon levels.

Our Mitigation Systems

  • Sub-slab Depressurization: Most common and effective method
  • Crawl Space Ventilation: For homes with crawl spaces
  • Block Wall Suction: For homes with hollow block foundations
  • Sump Hole Suction: Utilizes existing sump pump systems

System Components

  • High-performance radon fans (Fantech, RadonAway)
  • PVC piping system
  • Manometer for system monitoring
  • Warning labels and system identification

Our Guarantee

We guarantee to reduce radon levels below 4.0 pCi/L or we'll modify the system at no additional cost. All systems come with a comprehensive warranty.

AeroSeal Duct Sealing

Revolutionary technology to seal your ductwork from the inside

Advanced Duct Sealing Technology

AeroSeal is a patented technology that seals ductwork from the inside out. This innovative process can seal up to 90% of duct leaks, improving your home's energy efficiency and indoor air quality.

How AeroSeal Works

  • Pressurize your duct system
  • Inject safe, non-toxic sealant particles
  • Particles automatically find and seal leaks
  • Real-time monitoring shows sealing progress

Benefits

  • Reduce energy bills by up to 30%
  • Improve indoor air quality
  • Enhance comfort in all rooms
  • Reduce dust and allergens
  • 10-year warranty on sealed ducts

The Process

Our certified technicians use computer-controlled equipment to measure duct leakage before and after sealing, providing you with a detailed report of the improvements.
