Professional Radon Testing Services

Protect your family with accurate radon testing

Why Radon Testing Matters

Radon is a colorless, odorless gas that can accumulate in your home and pose serious health risks. Our professional radon testing services provide accurate measurements to help you make informed decisions about your family's safety.

Our Testing Process

  • Professional-grade testing equipment
  • 48-72 hour testing period for accurate results
  • Detailed report with recommendations
  • EPA-certified testing procedures

What You Get

  • Comprehensive radon level analysis
  • Written report within 24 hours
  • Mitigation recommendations if needed
  • Peace of mind for your family

About Us

At Lifetime Home Services, we believe your home should be as hardworking, comfortable, and personalized as the people who live in it. That's why we've brought together three of Southeast Wisconsin's most trusted home service companies—each with decades of experience—under one roof to offer a full suite of solutions for the modern homeowner.

Our Story

Our story begins over 25 years ago with Lifetime Radon Solutions, a locally-owned company dedicated to keeping Wisconsin families safe through professional radon testing and mitigation. Meanwhile, Closet Concepts and America In-Home were also building loyal followings—Closet Concepts by designing beautiful, custom storage systems for homes across the region, and America In-Home by pioneering whole-home automation, audio/video systems, and smart technology integration.

These three family-driven businesses have deep roots in the community and have long shared a simple philosophy: deliver high-quality, honest service that stands the test of time. In 2025, we joined forces to offer something even more powerful—a single, seamless source for expert solutions that enhance how people live, work, and feel in their homes.

What We Offer

What was once known as the Brookfield Idea Center—a collaborative space where local businesses showcased innovation—has now evolved into a new kind of one-stop shop. Under the Lifetime Home Services banner, we proudly offer:

  • Radon Testing & Mitigation
  • Epoxy Floor Coatings
  • Duct Cleaning & Sealing
  • Smart Home Automation & Whole-Home Networking
  • Custom Closets & Storage Solutions

And the best part? All the original owners and teams are still here, working side-by-side to bring you the same trusted service with even more value. We're local, we're experienced, and we're here to serve—with integrity, transparency, and a commitment to excellence that spans generations.

Welcome to Lifetime. Trusted Solutions. Lifetime Results.
