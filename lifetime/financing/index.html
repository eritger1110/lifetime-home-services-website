Financing FAQ

Common questions about our financing options and application process.

Synchrony Financial considers applications from borrowers with various credit profiles. While a higher credit score improves your chances of approval and better terms, they evaluate multiple factors including income, debt-to-income ratio, and employment history. Apply to see what options are available to you.

Most applications receive an instant decision. In some cases, additional verification may be required, which can take 1-2 business days. Once approved, you can schedule your project immediately.

There are no application fees, origination fees, or prepayment penalties. You can pay off your loan early without any additional charges. The only fees are late payment fees if payments are not made on time.

You'll need your Social Security number, annual income, employment information, and basic personal details. The application takes just 2-3 minutes to complete online.
