What It Is (Explain Like I'm 10)

Duct cleaning is like giving your home's breathing system a deep clean. Over time, dust, pet hair, and other stuff builds up in the tubes (ducts) that carry air around your house. We use special equipment to clean all that out.

Aeroseal duct sealing is like fixing tiny holes in those same tubes. We use a special technology that finds and seals small leaks from the inside, so your heating and cooling works better and doesn't waste energy.

The Bottom Line: Clean, sealed ducts mean better air quality, lower energy bills, and more comfortable temperatures throughout your home.

Aeroseal Duct Sealing Technology

Aeroseal is a breakthrough technology that seals duct leaks from the inside using a safe, non-toxic aerosol sealant.

How Aeroseal Works:
  • Computer-controlled process measures and maps all leaks
  • Safe sealant particles are blown through the duct system
  • Particles stick to leak edges and build up to seal holes
  • Real-time monitoring shows sealing progress
  • Final report shows before/after leak measurements
Aeroseal Benefits:
  • Seals leaks up to 5/8 inch in diameter
  • Reduces duct leakage by up to 90%
  • Improves energy efficiency by 20-30%
  • UL tested and approved for safety
  • 10-year warranty on sealed leaks

Duct Cleaning Process

Our Professional Cleaning Method

1
System Inspection

We inspect your entire duct system, identify problem areas, and document the current condition with photos.

2
Containment Setup

We set up negative pressure containment to prevent debris from spreading throughout your home during cleaning.

3
Agitation & Removal

Using specialized brushes and compressed air tools, we agitate and dislodge debris from duct surfaces.

4
HEPA Extraction

Powerful HEPA-filtered vacuum systems extract all loosened debris and contaminants from the duct system.

5
Component Cleaning

We clean registers, grilles, and accessible HVAC components for complete system cleanliness.

6
Final Inspection

We conduct a final inspection and provide before/after photos showing the cleaning results.

Aeroseal Sealing Process

Advanced Duct Sealing Technology

1
Pre-Sealing Testing

We measure and map all duct leaks using computer-controlled testing equipment to establish baseline leakage.

2
System Preparation

We temporarily block registers and prepare the duct system for the Aeroseal sealing process.

3
Sealant Application

The Aeroseal system blows safe, non-toxic sealant particles through the ducts, which stick to leak edges.

4
Real-Time Monitoring

Computer monitoring tracks sealing progress in real-time, showing exactly how much leakage is being sealed.

5
Post-Sealing Verification

We test the system again to measure final leakage levels and verify sealing effectiveness.

6
Performance Report

You receive a detailed report showing before/after measurements and warranty information.

When You Need Duct Services

Signs You Need Duct Cleaning

  • Visible dust or debris around vents
  • Musty or stale odors from vents
  • Increased allergy or asthma symptoms
  • Excessive dust accumulation in home
  • Recent renovation or construction
  • Pet hair or dander concerns
  • Haven't cleaned ducts in 3-5 years

Signs You Need Duct Sealing

  • High energy bills
  • Uneven temperatures between rooms
  • HVAC system runs constantly
  • Dusty home despite regular cleaning
  • Difficulty maintaining comfortable temperature
  • Whistling sounds from ducts
  • Rooms that never get comfortable
