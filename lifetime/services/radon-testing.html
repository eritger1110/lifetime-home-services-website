What It Is (Explain Like I'm 10)

Radon is a gas that can seep into homes from the ground. You can't see it, smell it, or taste it—so the only way to know your levels is to test. We use professional methods to measure your home's air and give you a clear, easy-to-understand report.

Think of it like checking your home's "invisible air quality." Just like you might test your water or check your smoke detector, radon testing tells you if there's something you should know about the air your family breathes every day.

The Bottom Line: Testing is quick, simple, and gives you peace of mind. We place a device, wait the right amount of time, then give you clear results and honest guidance.

Myths vs Facts

Myths

  • "You can smell high radon levels"
  • "Testing once is enough forever"
  • "All test methods are the same"
  • "Only old homes have radon problems"
  • "Radon only affects basements"

Facts

  • Radon is completely odorless and invisible
  • Levels can change over time due to various factors
  • Professional methods provide more accurate results
  • New and old homes can both have radon issues
  • Radon can affect any level of your home

Prep & What to Expect

Before We Arrive

  • Keep windows and doors closed for 12 hours before testing
  • Turn off fans and air purifiers in the testing area
  • Choose the lowest livable level of your home for placement
  • Clear a small area away from walls, windows, and doors

Day of Testing

1
Arrival & Setup (15 minutes)

Our technician arrives, explains the process, and places the testing device in the optimal location.

2
Testing Period (48 hours)

The device runs automatically. Continue normal activities but keep windows closed and don't move the device.

3
Retrieval & Results (30 minutes)

We return to collect the device and provide your results with clear explanation of what they mean.

During the Test

  • Keep windows and exterior doors closed
  • Normal entry and exit is fine
  • Don't move or disturb the testing device
  • Continue normal HVAC operation
  • Avoid using fans or air purifiers near the device
