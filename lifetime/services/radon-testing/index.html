What is Radon and Why Test

Radon is a naturally occurring gas that can enter through the foundation. You can't see or smell it; long-term exposure is a health risk. Testing is the only way to know your level.

How Our Testing Works

  1. We place a calibrated monitor or approved test kit correctly and advise on closed-house conditions.
  2. After the test period, we retrieve the device and generate your report.
  3. We explain the results and, if elevated, outline next steps.

What You Receive

  • Professional setup and retrieval
  • Clear pass or actionable report
  • No-pressure consultation on options
