Our 4-Layer Coating System

Professional-grade application process ensures maximum durability and longevity.

Layer 1: Surface Preparation

Diamond grinding and acid etching to ensure proper adhesion. Critical for long-term performance.

Layer 2: Primer Coat

High-performance primer penetrates concrete pores and creates strong foundation bond.

Layer 3: Base Coat

100% solids epoxy provides chemical resistance and color. Applied in multiple thin coats.

Layer 4: Topcoat

Clear polyurethane or polyaspartic topcoat provides UV protection and enhanced durability.

Professional Application Process

Our certified technicians follow strict protocols to ensure perfect results every time.

1

Surface Preparation

We begin with thorough cleaning and diamond grinding to remove any existing coatings, stains, or surface imperfections. This critical step ensures proper adhesion and long-term performance. Any cracks or damage are repaired using specialized concrete repair compounds.

2

Moisture Testing

We test concrete moisture levels using calcium chloride tests. Moisture levels must be below 3 lbs/1000 sq ft/24 hours for proper epoxy adhesion. If moisture is too high, we apply moisture mitigation systems before proceeding.

3

Primer Application

High-performance primer is applied using professional spray equipment. The primer penetrates deep into the concrete pores, creating a strong chemical bond that prevents delamination and ensures long-term adhesion.

4

Base Coat Installation

100% solids epoxy base coat is applied in thin, even layers using professional squeegees and rollers. Multiple coats ensure uniform thickness and optimal chemical resistance. Color is added at this stage.

5

Decorative Elements

While the base coat is still wet, decorative vinyl flakes are broadcast across the surface. These flakes provide texture, slip resistance, and aesthetic appeal while hiding minor imperfections in the concrete.

6

Topcoat Protection

Clear polyurethane or polyaspartic topcoat is applied to seal the system. This layer provides UV protection, enhanced chemical resistance, and makes the floor easy to clean and maintain.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about floor coating installation and maintenance.

Most residential garage floors can be completed in 2-3 days. Day 1 involves surface preparation and primer application. Day 2 includes base coat and decorative flakes. Day 3 is topcoat application. Larger commercial projects may take 4-7 days depending on square footage and complexity.

You can walk on the floor after 24 hours. Light foot traffic is fine after 48 hours. For vehicle traffic, we recommend waiting 72 hours for standard epoxy systems, or 24 hours for fast-cure polyaspartic systems. Full chemical resistance develops over 7 days.

Epoxy coatings offer excellent chemical resistance and are more cost-effective. They cure slowly, allowing more working time but requiring longer wait times. Polyaspartic coatings cure much faster (same-day installation possible), have superior UV resistance, and can be applied in colder temperatures, but cost more.

Maintenance is simple: sweep regularly and mop with mild soap and water. Avoid abrasive cleaners or steel wool. For tough stains, use a soft brush with warm soapy water. The non-porous surface resists most stains and chemicals, making cleanup easy.

We provide a 5-year warranty against delamination, peeling, or premature wear under normal use. The warranty covers material defects and installation issues but excludes damage from chemical spills, mechanical damage, or improper maintenance.

Existing coatings must be completely removed for proper adhesion. We use diamond grinding equipment to remove old paint, sealers, or previous coatings. Attempting to coat over existing materials will result in poor adhesion and premature failure.

We test all concrete for moisture vapor transmission before coating. For basements with moisture issues, we apply specialized moisture mitigation primers or vapor barrier systems. Proper moisture control is essential for coating longevity.
